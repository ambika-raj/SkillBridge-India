How it works

From zero to job-ready
in four steps

A simple journey designed for every Indian learner, regardless of background or education level.

Create your profile
Sign up as a student, instructor, or employer. Set your goals and let SkillBridge personalize your journey.
Learn with purpose
Enroll in government-recognized courses tied to PMKVY, NAPS, and Skill India schemes. Learn at your own pace.
Earn certificates
Complete courses to receive verified certificates with unique IDs. Each certificate is verifiable by employers on the platform.
Get hired
Apply for jobs with your SkillBridge certificates auto-attached. Employers can verify your skills directly on the platform.
